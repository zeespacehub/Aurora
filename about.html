The Main Focus

Who We Are

The Aurora Wings Initiative is a non-profit organization based in Nigeria, committed to empowering women and girls through a holistic approach that encompasses economic empowerment, education, and advocacy for gender equality.
The organization aims to break down systemic barriers, foster leadership, and promote well-being among women and girls.
The Aurora Wings Initiative envisions a Nigeria where women and girls are not only empowered but also respected and given equal opportunities to thrive.

Vision

A Nigeria where every girl and woman is empowered, respected, and equipped to reach their full potential, contributing to a society that values gender equality and justice for all.
Our Mission

Dedicated to empowering girls and women in Nigeria by economic independence, promoting educational opportunities, and advocacy for their rights, fostering a supportive environment for leadership, well-being and gender equality.

About Our Initiative

Thematic Areas of Aurora Wings Initiative

The Aurora Wings Initiative focuses on several key thematic areas to advance its mission of empowering women and girls in Nigeria. These thematic areas provide guidance for the organization's programs and initiatives, ensuring a comprehensive approach to addressing the challenges faced by women and girls.

1. Economic Empowerment:

Aurora Wings Initiative provides women and girls with the skills, resources, and opportunities to achieve economic independence. The organization offers vocational training, entrepreneurship support, and financial literacy programs to break the cycle of poverty and create pathways to sustainable livelihoods. Programs: - Vocational skills training in various trades - Entrepreneurship support and small business development - Financial literacy workshops and savings schemes
2. Education and Lifelong Learning Skills:

Aurora Wings Initiative enhances access to quality education for girls and young women by providing scholarships, mentoring, and literacy programs from primary to higher education levels. Programs: - Scholarship and mentorship programs for girls - After-school learning centers to improve academic performance - Digital literacy and STEM education programs for girls
3. Gender Rights Advocacy and Protection:

Aurora Wings Initiative advocates for the rights of women and girls, addressing critical issues like gender-based violence, child marriage, and reproductive rights through awareness campaigns, policy influence, and support services for victims. Programs: - Campaigns against gender-based violence and early marriage - Legal aid and support services for survivors of abuse - Reproductive rights advocacy and education on sexual health
4. Leadership Development:

Aurora Wings Initiative provides leadership training, mentorship, and capacity-building programs to equip girls and women with the skills they need to take on leadership roles in their communities and workplaces.
Programs:
- Leadership and governance training for young women - Mentorship programs linking girls with female role models - Workshops on public speaking, advocacy, and decision-making
5. Health and Well-being:

Aurora Wings Initiative ensures access to healthcare services, particularly maternal and reproductive health care, while also promoting mental health awareness and well-being. Programs: - Maternal health and child care services - Mental health awareness campaigns and counseling - Reproductive health education and services
6. Community Development and Partnerships:

Aurora Wings Initiative works with local communities, government bodies, and international organizations to create lasting impact through sustainable development projects and strategic partnerships. Programs: - Community-based projects focused on infrastructure, WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene), and social services - Partnerships with local, national, and international stakeholders - Community mobilization and capacity-building initiatives
7. Advocacy and Awareness Campaigns:
